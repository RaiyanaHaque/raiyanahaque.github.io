Arcane Messenger

Arcane Messenger is an endless runner game where the player plays as a girl who is on a mission to get a wizard who can potentially save her village from destruction and she was chosen as the arcane messenger to carry out this objective.

  • This was an independent project.
  • Done in Unity, C#.
  • Other softwares used: Photoshop and aesprite.
  • For this project, I tried to implement a shop system and an add revenue system for the game but was not able to finish it.
  • Grade achieved: Merit
