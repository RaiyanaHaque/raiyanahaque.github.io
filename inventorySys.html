RPG Inventory System Project

In my previous games, I wanted to create an inventory system but due to the lack of time I wasn't able to do it so I did a separate project on it solely looking at the mechanics of the system. It has the basic equip and unequip function although the armor cannot actually be seen on the character. Players can also see the stats of the equipment when the hover over it and they can also see what is modifying the character stats. There are also percentage modifiers that increases the stats of the base value. I also created a function for them to pick up objects from the environment. When anything was equipped it would go to its corresponding slot in character panel because of tags.
